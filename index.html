Data Security and Neutrality

NeutronMail is incorporated in Somewhere and all our servers are located in Switzerland. This means all user data is protected by strict Swiss privacy laws.

All emails are secured automatically with end-to-end encryption. This means even we can not decrypt and read your emails. As a result, your encrypted emails can not be shared with third parties.
